Delivery

When deadline comes we contact you and establish the way of delivery.
In case of print-purposed project we arrange sending file to you, and to the printing house (if needed). We provide quality check of the printouts so if the artwork is to be printed directly for you (flyer, brochure), we contact printers for proofs, and after the whole work is done, we check again if everything was printed correctly. In case of magazine ad or other artwork designed for print or web publication we send files to the publisher and ask for confirmation of compliance with their technical reqiurements.
We believe in creating interest among our clients through creating opportunity to save money, so we arrange print job for you and negotiate price, but we do not charge you for that, invoice from the printers will be sent directly to you. We have our base of reliable printers but we can of course cooperate with the printer of your choice. If you want us to choose the printer for you, part of the job is negotiating price and presenting it to you for approval.

When we deal with web project, we deliver the files to you, place it on your server or offer you some additional services.
