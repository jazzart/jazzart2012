How we can lower your design costs?

The main idea is something we called direct invoicing.
Let's explain it by example - you need well designed advertising campaign for print and web. When you turn to Advertising Agency their Manager is assigning Designer and Art Director to your project. When the product is ready, Agency sends it to the printers, then returns it to you, invoicing for the design, for the work of all people involved and to add something else - they also usually take some profit margin on printer's work. Not to mention that you have to pay for enormous amout of coffee drunk while braistorming on your project.

Well - with us you only pay for the design and the coffee! If we deal with project for print, we ask printers to give you your own separate invoice.
We will negotiate price for you, and we will do it as part of the job, without any additional costs. We do not have account managers and key account managers, so if we sign our one-time contract for the job, you will quickly see that you are not paying the whole team of people, but directly one designer (for his/her work and the coffee).
