About me

Through my almost 10 years career I have been working on positions of all industry levels – from DTP Specialist to Graphic Designer, Web Designer and Art Director, mostly in English spoken, international environment. I have gained experience in worldwide-operating companies and workplaces in several cities in Europe. In my portfolio you can find layout and graphic design, brand and company identification, advertising campaigns, catalogues, magazine layouts and web design: front-end, web layouts and newsletter campaigns.

I strongly believe in the principle that you can only be good when you are doing something you really like. My occupation emerged from passion to photography and graphics and evolved into skills I can firmly use to deliver professional results. I am trying to always stay open to new technologies and styles of design, constantly developing my skills – right now in web development direction.
